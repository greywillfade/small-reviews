We've had this game on the go a _long_ time – on the Xbox since August last year, and I think it started getting played by my kid and husband on the Playstation before that. It pops up now and again in the kid's rotation of interest, but given I'm sat inside pretty immobile I was compelled to finally 100% it and consider it personally 'done'. 

The game came out in 2014, and so obviously is of that era and doesn't stand up in the same way against Batman Legacy. It's pretty mindless fun in the same way that all the Lego games are, with some grind and tedium, some bugs, some confusing mechanisms, and a lot of annoying replaying to clean up the last collectables. That said, I _did_ finish it all, but there's no way I'm paying even a few quid for the DLC to extend it even more!
